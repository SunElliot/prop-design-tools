📖 How to use

  1. Enter the spacecraft mass, thrust, Isp and this maneuver's Δv (one item of the Δv Budget).输入整星质量、推力、Isp 与本次机动的 Δv（Δv 预算中的一项）。
  2. The burn-arc check flags burns longer than ~5% of the orbit — impulsive assumptions break down and gravity losses grow; split the maneuver or use a bigger thruster.点火弧段检查提示超过轨道周期约 5% 的长点火——脉冲假设失效、引力损失增大；应分次点火或加大推力。
  3. Total impulse across all maneuvers drives thruster qualification (life, pulse count) — sum it over the mission.全任务总冲决定推力器鉴定要求（寿命、脉冲次数）——请对整个任务累加。

Burn relations

Propellant from the rocket equation: m_p = m₀·(1 − e^(−Δv/Isp·g₀)); mass flow ṁ = F/(Isp·g₀); burn time t = m_p/ṁ; total impulse I = F·t = m_p·Isp·g₀.

Finite-burn (gravity) losses stay below ~1% while the burn arc is under a few percent of the orbit; long spiral-like burns should be analyzed as low-thrust transfers instead (see the EP tool).

All computation runs locally in your browser.
